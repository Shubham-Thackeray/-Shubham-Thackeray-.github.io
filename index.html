Description

The Aditya Birla Onboarding App is specially designed for newly joined members. A complete gamified experience to the onboarding journey. Complete modules, play games and explore the group in a gamified way!

It covers five Major Modules in which the Aditya Birla Group is divided, each module contains the number of companies and the complete details about each, after each module, there is a quiz experience to check the user knowledge and award them on achievement screen.  As Abg group is much bigger the app contains 50+ screens in total

The app also covers three major games specially developed to add a little bit of gaming experience. Those games are Logo_motion, Connection and How much you know, each has a great user interface.

It also contains character animations which popups on the screen to give user information throughout the journey. It contains two selectable characters, users can select one on the welcome screen and the characters animate to deliver useful information throughout the app.

Description

After watching the tremendous success of Netflix and Amazon, Sony also held a chance to grab the digital Market by introducing Sony Liv as a website and as a mobile app too. As everyone knows the Amitabh Bacchan famed Kaun Banega crorepati (KBC) is very popular in India, Someone from the KBC core team got up with a playAlong idea , where sony liv users will get a platform to answer questions while the game is still going on the television, taking every device to sync with television. It got another component like the scoreboard, offline quiz, most asked questions and various backend integrable components for the paid promotions on the sony liv app.

1. To achieve the real-time playAlong game we had to use the socket connection along with the robustness to maintain millions of connections, we integrated the LightStreamer. 

2. We also had to make revenue from the AdSense as the sony Networks believe in making each rupee count. So to fulfill the requirement we had to use paid CMS from the BrightCove. Which gave us the advantage to maintain the millions of ad hits at a time with minimal errors on our video ads. 

3. The sony_liv app is native and we coded in angular to inject R files to native SDK and IOS native but that is not all, for minimizing time complexity we made native functions to hit the web APIS and we used to get that data from the native app to a web view using our angular scripts. 

4. The final website had few more issues to track the redFlag as the IOS and browsers are nontrackable so we had to fingerprint the device from the network.

5. we also faced the timer issue as IOS devices and web apps were restricting the background threading. We had to trigger various events to make timer work.

6. Another issue was the autoplay functionality on the web app which we noticed on the live portal, as per the new chrome terms, autoplay of video was restricted so we had to bypass the chrome. algorithm to make the videos work.

7. The most annoying development issue was the deadline, we had to work really smart to complete it before the deadline.
